## Overview

Australia has a growing technology and innovation ecosystem. The federal government's digital economy strategies aim to position Australia as a leading digital economy by 2030. Key tech hubs are concentrated in Sydney (CBD & Pyrmont), Melbourne (Richmond/Cremorne "Silicon Yarra"), Brisbane, and Canberra.

## Artificial Intelligence

### AI Ethics
Australia's **AI Ethics Principles** (2019, DISR) define 8 principles for responsible AI:
1. Human, societal, and environmental wellbeing
2. Human-centred values
3. Fairness
4. Privacy protection and security
5. Reliability and safety
6. Transparency and explainability
7. Contestability
8. Accountability

**Source:** [industry.gov.au/ai-ethics](https://www.industry.gov.au/publications/australias-artificial-intelligence-ethics-principles)

## CSIRO — Commonwealth Scientific and Industrial Research Organisation

- **URL:** [csiro.au](https://www.csiro.au)

## Connectivity Infrastructure

### National Broadband Network (NBN)
The NBN is Australia's national wholesale broadband network; a mix of Fibre to the Premises (FTTP), Hybrid Fibre-Coaxial (HFC), and Fixed Wireless technologies.
- **Operator:** NBN Co
- **URL:** [nbnco.com.au](https://www.nbnco.com.au)
- **Regulator:** Australian Communications and Media Authority (ACMA)
