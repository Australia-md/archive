## Overview

**Alstonville** (postcode 2477) is a mixed residential and commercial suburb in the Ballina local government area in the Northern Rivers region of New South Wales. It sits on the Bruxner Highway approximately 13 km west of Ballina CBD and 19 km east of Lismore. The 2021 Census recorded a population of 5,739 people in Alstonville, and the suburb functions as a hinterland service centre for surrounding communities including Wollongbar and the wider Ballina plateau.

All dental practitioners listed below are required to hold current registration with the **Dental Board of Australia** under the Health Practitioner Regulation National Law. Registration status for any individual practitioner can be verified at [Dental Board of Australia](https://www.dentalboard.gov.au) by clicking "Look up practitioners".
