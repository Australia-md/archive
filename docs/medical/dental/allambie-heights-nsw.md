## Overview

**Allambie Heights** (postcode 2100) is a residential suburb in the Northern Beaches Council local government area, approximately 17.5 km north-east of the Sydney CBD. It forms part of Sydney's Northern Beaches region and had a population of 7,317 at the 2021 Census. The suburb's main business area is centred near the local community centre and primary school, while Manly Dam Reserve and Garigal National Park sit on its southern and western edges. These local services support residents of Allambie Heights as well as nearby Northern Beaches suburbs.

All dental practitioners listed below are required to hold current registration with the **Dental Board of Australia** under the Health Practitioner Regulation National Law. Registration status for any individual practitioner can be verified at [Dental Board of Australia](https://www.dentalboard.gov.au) by clicking "Look up practitioners".
