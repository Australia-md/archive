## Overview

**Albion Park** (postcode 2527) is a mixed residential and commercial suburb in the City of Shellharbour local government area, approximately 18 km south of Wollongong CBD. It sits in the Illawarra region and forms part of the wider Wollongong metropolitan area. The 2021 Census recorded a population of 13,826 people in Albion Park. The suburb's role as a long-established town centre on Tongarra Road supports local access to general dental, restorative, cosmetic, and emergency dental services.

All dental practitioners listed below are required to hold current registration with the **Dental Board of Australia** under the Health Practitioner Regulation National Law. Registration status for any individual practitioner can be verified at [Dental Board of Australia](https://www.dentalboard.gov.au) by clicking "Look up practitioners".
