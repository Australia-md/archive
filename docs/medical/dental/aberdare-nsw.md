## Overview

**Aberdare** (postcode 2325) is a mixed residential suburb in the Cessnock City Council local government area in the Lower Hunter region of New South Wales. ABS and gazetted locality sources identify Aberdare as part of the Cessnock urban area, about 2.3 km east-south-east of central Cessnock, 48.2 km west-north-west of Newcastle, and 153 km north of Sydney. The 2021 Census recorded a population of 2,542 people in Aberdare. For residents, the suburb's dental access is closely tied to services in the Cessnock hospital and town-centre catchment.

All dental practitioners listed below are required to hold current registration with the **Dental Board of Australia** under the Health Practitioner Regulation National Law. Registration status for any individual practitioner can be verified at [Dental Board of Australia](https://www.dentalboard.gov.au) by clicking "Look up practitioners".
