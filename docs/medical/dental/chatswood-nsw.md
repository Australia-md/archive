## Medicare & Bulk Billing Notes

The **Child Dental Benefits Schedule (CDBS)** can cover part or the full cost of some basic dental services for eligible children aged 0 to 17. Bulk billing is largely unavailable in Chatswood: most clinics listed here charge fees. HCF Dental Centre Chatswood is listed as mixed billing with bulk billing available under specific conditions.
