## Overview

**Airds** (postcode 2560) is a predominantly residential suburb in the City of Campbelltown local government area, approximately 56 km south-west of the Sydney CBD. It forms part of Sydney's Macarthur region and had a population of 3,265 at the 2021 Census. Airds borders Campbelltown and nearby south-west Sydney suburbs including Bradbury, Ruse, Kentlyn and St Helens Park, placing local dental care within the broader Campbelltown service catchment. Local oral health access in the suburb is provided through Tharawal Aboriginal Corporation's dental clinic.

All dental practitioners listed below are required to hold current registration with the **Dental Board of Australia** under the Health Practitioner Regulation National Law. Registration status for any individual practitioner can be verified at [Dental Board of Australia](https://www.dentalboard.gov.au) by clicking "Look up practitioners".
