## Overview

**Albury** (postcode 2640) is a mixed residential and commercial suburb in the City of Albury local government area, on the northern side of the Murray River opposite Wodonga in Victoria. It lies approximately 326 km north-east of Melbourne CBD and about 554 km south-west of Sydney. Albury is a major regional service centre in southern New South Wales and forms the NSW side of the cross-border Albury–Wodonga urban area.

All dental practitioners listed below are required to hold current registration with the **Dental Board of Australia** under the Health Practitioner Regulation National Law. Registration status for any individual practitioner can be verified at [Dental Board of Australia](https://www.dentalboard.gov.au) by clicking "Look up practitioners".
