3 Verified Clinics · Albury · Postcode 2640 · AHPRA Regulated

Albury (postcode 2640) is a mixed residential and commercial suburb in the City of Albury local government area, on the northern side of the Murray River opposite Wodonga in Victoria. It lies approximately 326 km north-east of Melbourne CBD and about 554 km south-west of Sydney. Albury is a major regional service centre in southern New South Wales and forms the NSW side of the cross-border Albury–Wodonga urban area. Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify a dentist's AHPRA registration in Albury?

Visit the Dental Board of Australia website at www.dentalboard.gov.au and click "Look up practitioners". You can search by practitioner name to confirm current registration status, conditions, and disciplinary history.

Which dental clinics in Albury are open on weekends?

Albury Family Dental at 685 David Street offers Saturday appointments by arrangement. Sunday hours are not available. Contact the clinic directly on (02) 6048 7808 to confirm weekend availability.

Is there a public dental clinic in Albury?

Southern Health Albury Community Health Centre (Dental) at 596 Smollett Street provides public dental clinic services. Eligibility includes under-18s with a medical card and adults with a Centrelink card and Medicare card. Contact 1800 450 046 for details.
