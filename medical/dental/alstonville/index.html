Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify a dentist's AHPRA registration in Alstonville?

All dental practitioners in Australia must hold current registration with the Dental Board of Australia under the Health Practitioner Regulation National Law. Visit www.dentalboard.gov.au and click "Look up practitioners" to search by practitioner name and confirm registration status, conditions, and disciplinary history.

Which health funds do Alstonville dental clinics participate with?
