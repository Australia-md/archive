Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify a dentist's AHPRA registration in Alexandria?

Visit the Dental Board of Australia website at www.dentalboard.gov.au and click "Look up practitioners" to search by name. All dental practitioners in Australia must hold current registration under the Health Practitioner Regulation National Law.

Which dental clinics in Alexandria are open on weekends?

Dens in Dente Dental Care at Shop 2, 138-142 Botany Road is open Saturday and Sunday 8:00am to 4:00pm, and closed on public holidays. Alpha Health Clinic weekend hours were not confirmed from public sources at the time of verification.

Which Alexandria dental clinics offer emergency dental care?

Both Alpha Health Clinic and Dens in Dente Dental Care list emergency dentistry among their services. Contact each clinic directly for emergency availability and after-hours arrangements.
