Adamstown (postcode 2289) is a mixed residential and commercial suburb in the City of Newcastle / City of Lake Macquarie local government area, approximately 6 km west-south-west of the Newcastle CBD. It forms part of the Newcastle metropolitan area and had a population of 6,335 at the 2021 Census. Adamstown is served by Adamstown railway station on the Main North line and sits at the northern end of the Fernleigh Track, connecting it with surrounding Newcastle and Lake Macquarie communities.
