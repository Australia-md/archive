Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify a dentist's AHPRA registration in Macquarie Park?

All dental practitioners in Australia must hold current registration with the Dental Board of Australia under AHPRA. Visit www.dentalboard.gov.au and click the "Look up practitioners" button to search by name and verify registration status.

Which dental clinics in Macquarie Park are open on weekends?

Wise Dental (11 Khartoum Rd) is open 7 days a week, 10:00am–10:00pm. MacPark Dental (50 Waterloo Rd) is open Saturday 9:00am–5:00pm. North Ryde Dentistry (64 Talavera Rd) is open Saturday 8:30am–4:00pm.

Which Macquarie Park dental clinics offer emergency dental care?

Wise Dental at 11 Khartoum Rd, Macquarie Park operates a walk-in emergency dental clinic, open 7 days a week from 10:00am to 10:00pm. For after-hours dental emergencies, contact the clinic directly on (02) 9216 7676.

Is there a dental clinic near Macquarie Park Metro Station?

MacPark Dental at Suite 3, Level 2, 50 Waterloo Rd is a 2-minute walk from Macquarie Park Metro Station and also offers on-site parking.
